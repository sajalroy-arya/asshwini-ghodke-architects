Our Philosophy

Creating timeless architecture that reflects how people truly live.

At Asshwini Ghodke Architects Interiors & Associates, we believe that space should tell a story. Architecture is not merely the assembly of materials, but the choreographing of light, shadow, and movement.

Based in Indore, our practice merges rigorous intellectual research with hands-on craft. We design customized environments that balance artistic vision with functional clarity.

Innovation

Pushing material and structural boundaries to deliver custom architectural identities.

Functionality

Space planned ergonomically around your lifestyle, ensuring seamless daily experiences.

Budget-Conscious

Meticulous documentation and quantity surveying to align luxury design with financial parameters.

Premium Execution

Direct oversight and turnkey supervision to ensure absolute fidelity to the blueprints.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Every interior project is uniquely scoped. Costs depend entirely on the material specification, civil reconstruction requirements, and furniture designs. Our typical project budgets range from ₹10 Lakhs for premium single spaces or apartments up to ₹2 Crores+ for extensive luxury villa turnkeys. We provide a detailed Bill of Quantities (BOQ) during the design phase to outline costs clearly.

A typical residential apartment project takes between 3 to 5 months. Architectural design and execution of custom luxury villas can range from 8 to 14 months depending on structural scale and turnkey details. A detailed schedule with project phases is provided prior to commencement.

Yes, we specialize in turnkey execution. This means we supervise the design concept phase, coordinate with civil contracting teams, procure materials directly from manufacturers, and supervise structural assemblies, providing you a seamless transition from blueprints to key handover.

While our primary studio is based in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, we design and supervise architecture and luxury residential villa projects across India. For outstation projects, we conduct initial site visits and deliver detailed construction layouts, coordinating with local partners or placing site supervisors.
